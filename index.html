Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Memory Keeper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Local Data Only: Our app operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device. We collect and store:

• Photos and images you choose to add to your memories

• Captions and notes you write for your memories

• Date information for organizing your memories

No Cloud Storage: We do not upload, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers or cloud services. All your memories remain on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

• Display your memories in the app

• Organize your photos by date

• Generate videos from your photos (processed locally on your device)

• Enable daily reminders (if you choose to enable them)

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.

4. Device Permissions

To provide core functionality, our app may request access to:

• Photo Library: To allow you to select and save photos

• Camera: To allow you to take photos directly in the app

• Notifications: To send daily reminders (optional, can be disabled)

All permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

• Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features

• We do not have access to your data

• Your data cannot be accessed by third parties through our app

We recommend using device encryption and secure lock screens to protect your data.

6. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

• You can delete individual memories at any time

• You can clear all app data using the "Clear Local Data" option in Settings

• Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app store listing or your preferred method of contact.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your data (all data is stored locally on your device)

• Delete your data at any time

• Export your photos using the export feature in Settings

• Control app permissions through your device settings

By using Memory Keeper, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
